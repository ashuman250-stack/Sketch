Essence of Psychology

  • Psychology is the scientific study of mind and behavior.
  • It helps understand thoughts, emotions, and actions.
  • Major goals: describe, explain, predict, and control behavior.
  • Branches include cognitive, developmental, social, and clinical psychology.

Sensation and Perception

  • Sensation: the process of detecting physical stimuli from environment.
  • Perception: interpretation of sensory information to form understanding.
  • Sensory systems include vision, hearing, touch, taste, and smell.
  • Perceptual processes involve attention, organization, and interpretation.
